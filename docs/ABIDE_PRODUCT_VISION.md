# Abide Product Vision

## Mission

Abide helps Christians reflect on how God is transforming their character through the Fruit of the Spirit.

The app is not primarily a Bible app, prayer app, or habit tracker.

Its purpose is to help users answer a simple question:

> How is God changing me?

## Core Philosophy

Abide focuses on transformation rather than activity tracking.

## MVP

### Daily Fruit Reflection

Users complete one reflection per day.

No morning check-in.
No evening check-in.
No streaks.
No gamification.

### Reflection Structure

1. What happened today?
2. Which fruits were present?
3. Rooted In (optional scripture reference)
4. What is God teaching you?

### Fruits

- Love
- Joy
- Peace
- Patience
- Kindness
- Goodness
- Faithfulness
- Gentleness
- Self-Control

## Dashboard

The dashboard should answer:

> How am I growing?

### Sections

- Reflection status
- Recent reflections
- Fruit trends
- Growth highlights

## Fruit Timeline

The timeline is the heart of the application.

Each reflection becomes part of a long-term record of spiritual growth and transformation.

## Future Features

- Guided fruit focus
- AI reflection companion
- Prayer integration
- Testimony builder

## What Abide Is Not

- Bible reading plan app
- Streak tracker
- Habit tracker
- Gamified Christian app
- Social network

## Success Criteria

Users should be able to recognise patterns of growth, connect experiences to Scripture, and build a lasting record of spiritual transformation.
